    MISSION | Living Missionally in our Neighborhoods

    A four-week class exploring our theology and practices of mission in New Haven.

    Sundays March 15-April 19
    9:30am During Sunday Studies
    Fellowship Hall Downstairs at CPC

    What does it mean to live missionally in our neighborhoods, relationships, and God-given spheres? This class explores a theology of mission and practical ways to practice hospitality, presence, and gospel witness in New Haven.

    For more on our theology of mission, see Total Christ class #2, Missional.
