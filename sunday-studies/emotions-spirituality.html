    Adult Sunday Studies

    HEART | Emotions & Spirituality

    A four-week class exploring the connection between our faith and our feelings.

    Sundays April 26-May 17
    9:30am During Sunday Studies
    Fellowship Hall Downstairs at CPC

    What do my emotions have to do with my faith? Does God care about my feelings? Should I always have positive feelings for God, and what should I do when I don't?

    This class will discuss emotionally healthy spirituality, Christian transformation, and sanctification. All are welcome.
