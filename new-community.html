Community

Small Groups

Life Groups

God's people are the hands and feet of Christ. We cannot experience God and grow spiritually in a truly gospel-centered and biblical way if we are living in isolation. Neither can we say we are flourishing if our brother or sister in Christ is suffering.

Life Groups are our way of doing church in the small—with prayer, teaching, fellowship, and care—helping participants experience Christ outside of our worship gatherings.

Discipleship Groups

Christian discipleship applies the gospel to every aspect of life. Our Discipleship Groups focus on living and sharing truth through accountability, study, and prayer.
