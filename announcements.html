General Announcements

Interested in Joining the Body of Christ at CPC?

We consider the church an essential element of the gospel, so joining CPC as a member is a way to truly join the body of Christ, in all its blessings and with all its flaws! Before joining, you'll meet one-on-one with a pastor and then discuss how you've come to know Christ with the Shepherd Leaders. Contact Alexis Peterson (admin@cpcnewhaven.org) to begin the process. Our next joining will take place on Sunday, October 13.
