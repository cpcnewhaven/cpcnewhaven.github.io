Pastor's Book Pick

Life Together

by Dietrich Bonhoeffer

A modern classic, we’re treading into deep waters with this one! Written by pastor-theologian and martyr, Life Together is Bonhoeffer’s vision for the Christian life, both individually and communally.

Written during a time of great upheaval, this book remains a vital guide for understanding how we live together as the body of Christ. Copies are in the foyer - suggested donation of $15.
