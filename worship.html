Worship

As you enter the sanctuary, you'll be greeted by folks who will hand you a worship bulletin that will direct you through every step of our service including explanations of why we are carrying out each element.

We are located next to the Yale School of Management, at the corner of Whitney Ave. and Bradley St.

Sunday Morning Worship: We hold one service with communion at 10:30am. Masks are optional at CPC for everyone regardless of vaccination status. The Downstairs Sanctuary is reserved for those who prefer to wear a mask.

What to Wear: Attire ranges from t-shirts and jeans to more formal wear. Wear what makes you feel comfortable.

Parking: Limited parking on-site. Additional parking available on Bradley Street and at nearby Yale parking lots.

Worship: You'll receive a worship bulletin guiding you through the service.

A Safe Place: CPC is a welcoming and accepting community where all are encouraged to come as they are.

Children: Children are welcome in worship. Childcare available if needed.

Welcome Cards: Fill out a Welcome Card for follow-up and support.

Children's Sunday School & Adult Studies: Classes available before worship.

Fellowship Lunch: Shared meal after service on most Sundays.

Enjoy: Pick up our Welcome Brochure for more information and feel free to ask questions.
