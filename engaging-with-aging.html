Engaging with Aging

January 25, 2025

9:00 AM - 12:30 PM

CPC Fellowship Hall

We're having a seminar and discussion on issues related to aging well, end-of-life, and death. These will include a biblical and theological survey from Pastor Craig, a presentation and Q&A with a Geriatric physician, as well as legal and financial suggestions that everyone should be aware of. All ages are welcome since this will impact everyone at some point! Light breakfast and refreshments provided, and it will be livestreamed.
