Mercy Days Registration

March 29th and August 23rd

To truly love and serve one another, we'll tackle projects for those who need any help. Each project will have a dedicated project lead/captain who will give instructions to volunteers.

Previous Projects Include:

  • Clean-Out Basement
  • Touch-Up Living Room
  • Move Soil from Cleared Land
  • Sand Paint off Deck

This is an opportunity to be vulnerable and walk with one another, and even though that might initially be uncomfortable, it is planting the seeds and laying the foundation for deeper, more intimate relationships with our brothers and sisters.

To volunteer or request a Mercy Project, please fill out this form. You can reach out to Kaitlyn Jesse directly (kaijessee@gmail.com) with any questions.
