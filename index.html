NEW HERE?

Say hi! We’d love to get to know you. Fill out a Welcome Card from the pew and return it to the Welcome Bar in the foyer, and pick up a gift just for you! Check out the Connect Board in the foyer for opportunities to get to know other members of the congregation.

WEEKLY FELLOWSHIP MEALS RESUME TODAY

Join us for a fellowship lunch after worship every Sunday! We hope you can stay and get to know one-another. If you’re new, we’d love to meet you and this is a great opportunity to do so! Suggested donation of $5 per meal, maximum of $20 per household (guests and kids eat free). Don't let costs hinder you from joining, we'd love to have you come anyway!

SUNDAY FUNDAY PLANNING DURING LUNCH TODAY

Sunday Funday planning is underway and we'd love to have you join our team! Help CPC welcome newcomers to New Haven, celebrate the beginning of a new season, and enjoy our family fellowship after worship with fun and food for all generations. We'll meet to discuss the vision and logistics of the event today after worship during lunch (Sunday Funday itself is 9/10). Anyone is welcome, just show up and meet Colleen Rice in the Lecture Room with your lunch!

LAST DAY TO REGISTER FOR I HEART NEW HAVEN DAY IS TODAY

As a capstone to our CPC Impact Summer, join Bridges of Hope (BOH) with other churches throughout Greater New Haven in a day of service to our city on Saturday 8/26. This year we will be gathering at the New Haven Green for worship at 9 AM and then dispersing to sites across the city for service from 10 AM until approximately 2 PM. Families are welcome! Please register each member of your family separately, as a waiver is needed for each one.

  • Learn more and sign up to volunteer online at: cpcnewhaven.org/impact
  • Deadline to register: TODAY Sunday 8/20!
  • Register on-site today: see Tyler Rice in the foyer!

CONNECT BOARD: Want to join or share some fun this summer? Text CONNECT to 860-467-0010 (or go to connect.cpcnewhaven.org). PASTORAL COUNSELING AVAILABLE: Would you like to speak one-on-one with a CPC pastor? We desire to connect the truths of scripture to the realities of life, whether you are going through suffering or just need wisdom. Sign-up confidentially at cpcnewhaven.org/pastoral-counseling or text PASTORCONSULT to 860-467-0010.
BOH MENTORING PROGRAM | 2023-2024 SCHOOL YEAR: Invest in the life of a New Haven child and their family with Bridges of Hope (BOH)! Our vision is to provide a safe place for students from low-income families to receive academic help and grow emotionally and spiritually alongside a caring adult. Volunteers should be over 18 and willing to commit one night a week to spending time with a 1st-8th grader from 5:15-7pm. Duties will include homework help, short devotions, and small group recreation. Mentors agree to attend 4 hours of training and orientation in early Sept. and Jan., and communicate regularly with their mentee's parents. Contact Rafa (rafael.luna@bridgesnewhaven.org) or go to: bridgesnewhaven.org/mentoring
